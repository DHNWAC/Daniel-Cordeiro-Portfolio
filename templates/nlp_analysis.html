NLP Analysis

Leveraging natural language processing to extract insights from product reviews and customer feedback

Project Overview

This project demonstrates NLP techniques to analyse customer reviews through sentiment analysis, topic modelling and other methodologies.

Business Objectives

  • Assess overall customer sentiment for different products
  • Identify specific product aspects (e.g., battery, display, performance) that receive positive or negative feedback
  • Discover key topics and themes in customer reviews
Technologies Used
Python NLTK Pandas NumPy Matplotlib Seaborn scikit-learn WordCloud
Techniques
Sentiment Analysis Topic modelling Aspect-Based Sentiment Text Preprocessing N-gram Analysis Word Frequency NMF

Methodology

Analytical Approach
  1. Text Preprocessing: Cleaning, tokenization, stopword removal, and lemmatization to prepare text for analysis
  2. Sentiment Analysis: Using VADER to quantify the positive, negative, or neutral sentiment in each review
  3. Word Clouds: Visualization of frequently mentioned terms for different sentiment categories
  4. N-gram Analysis: Identifying common phrases and word pairs to understand context
  5. Topic modelling: Using Non-negative Matrix Factorization (NMF) to identify key themes in reviews
  6. Aspect-Based Sentiment Analysis: Extracting sentiment about specific product features
  7. Review Length Analysis: Examining the relationship between review length and sentiment/helpfulness

Key Findings

1. Product Ratings and Overall Sentiment

Analysis of ratings and sentiment scores revealed significant differences in customer satisfaction across products. While ratings and sentiment are correlated, sentiment analysis identified nuanced opinions not captured by star ratings alone.

4. Topic modelling

Non-negative Matrix Factorization (NMF) was used to identify 5 distinct topics in the review corpus, providing insights into the main themes and concerns expressed by customers.

5. Aspect-Based Sentiment Analysis

By analyzing sentiment specifically for different product aspects (features), we gained detailed insights into which features customers liked or disliked across products.

Key Aspect-Based Insights
  • Battery: Most frequently mentioned aspect (236 mentions), with mixed sentiment (-0.12 for Budget Phone, +0.65 for Tablet Mini)
  • Display: Second most mentioned aspect (214 mentions), generally positive sentiment (+0.47 average)
  • Performance: Wide variation in sentiment across products (+0.72 for Laptop Pro, -0.23 for Budget Phone)
  • Price: Most negative aspect for premium products (-0.15 for Smartphone X), positive for Budget Phone (+0.58)
  • Camera: Highly polarized aspect (strongest positive for Smartphone X at +0.76, strongest negative for Budget Phone at -0.36)
  • Sound: Highest overall sentiment (+0.78) for Headphones Ultra

Conclusions & Recommendations

Key Product Insights

Product Strengths
  • Headphones Ultra: Industry-leading sound quality and comfort, excellent battery life (90% positive sentiment)
  • Smartphone X: Superior camera quality, premium display, decent battery life (80% positive sentiment)
  • Laptop Pro: Strong performance, high-quality display, good build quality (70% positive sentiment)
  • Tablet Mini: Excellent battery life, portable design, good display (60% positive sentiment)
Areas for Improvement
  • Budget Phone: Poor camera quality, performance issues, battery longevity (30% positive sentiment)
  • Smart Watch: Battery drain issues, limited functionality (50% positive sentiment)
  • Digital Camera: Mixed reviews on price-to-performance ratio (60% positive sentiment)
  • Smartphone X: Price concerns, software bugs mentioned in some reviews
