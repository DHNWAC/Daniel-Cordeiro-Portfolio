Time Series Analysis

Forecasting future sales trends using time series decomposition, anomaly detection, and Prophet modelling

Project Overview

Conducted a time series analysis to better understand the underling sales patters within a retain business. I did so by analysing historical sales data and attempted to uncover seasonal patterns and other hidden trends.

Business Objectives

  • Identify underlying patterns in sales data (trend, seasonality, cyclical components)
  • Generate accurate 60-day sales forecasts to support inventory and staffing decisions
Technologies Used
Python Pandas NumPy Matplotlib Seaborn StatsModels Prophet
Techniques
Time Series Decomposition Anomaly Detection Trend Analysis Seasonal Decomposition Prophet Forecasting Category Analysis

Methodology

Analytical Approach
  1. Exploratory Analysis: Visualizing time series components and patterns
  2. Time Series Decomposition: Separating trend, seasonality, and residual components
  3. Anomaly Detection: Identifying unusual sales patterns and events
  4. Category Analysis: Comparing performance across product categories
  5. Correlation Analysis: Understanding relationship with external factors
  6. Prophet Forecasting: Building predictive models for future sales

Key Findings

1. Overall Sales Trend

Sales data has a clear upward trend with stron seasonal patters in holiday peaks and and also has post holiday drops.

2. Time Series Decomposition

Decomposing the time series into its component parts in order to reveal some kind of underlying trend or seasonal patterns.

Decomposition Insights
  • Trend Component: Shows consistent growth
  • Seasonal Component: Clear weekly patterns
  • Residual Component: Mostly random noise

3. Anomaly Detection

Using statistical methods to identify anomalies in the sales data.

5. Sales Forecast

Using the Prophet forecasting model, I generated a 60-day forecast of future sales. The model ensures that the trends due to seasonality and holidays are captured.

Key Forecast Insights
  • Expected sales growth of 12.5% in the next 60 days compared to same period last year
  • Projected highest sales day: Black Friday (November 29, 2024)
  • Weekend sales expected to continue 25-30% higher than weekdays
  • Electronics category projected to lead growth at 18.3%

Conclusions & Recommendations

Key Business Insights

  1. Seasonal Patterns: Strong weekly cycles (weekends +25-35%) and yearly seasonality (Q4 holiday peaks) should drive inventory and staffing decisions.
  2. Special Events Impact: Promotions during key holidays generate significant sales spikes, with Black Friday producing nearly double normal daily revenue.
  3. Traffic-Sales Relationship: Website traffic drives sales with a 0.78 correlation, but conversion rate optimization opportunities exist to improve this relationship.
